Cedric Lancy is a French Mexican citizen that has done most of his education in the United States. He graduated with an associate of arts from Collin college and is now attending Brigham Young University. Cedric is a magician, graphic designer, and entrepreneur.

He is the creator and founder of Your Card Legacy which is a playing card company focus on selling luxury playing cards. During the 2020 pandemic, Cedric launched the Compass playing cards through Kickstarter. Thank to theKickstarter, he was able to get enough backing to start his own playing card design company.
